About Me

I am a second-year Master student of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. I am currently appliying for Ph.D. position in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.

I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Engineerging from Tsinghua University in 2015, advised by JianSheng Chen. I am going to receive my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering: Systems at University of Michigan - Ann Arbor in April, 2017.

During the summer of 2016, I worked as a Graduate Student Research Asssitant (GSRA) at Computer Science and Engineering, under the supervision of Professor Jia Deng and Professor Khurshid Ghani. During the summer of 2014, I was an visiting scholar at Duke University under the supervision of Professor Maciej Mazurowski.

Research Interest

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. To be specific, I found myself interested in making use of visual data available in vast amount to let machines autonomously understand and analyze the world around , with the aid of machine learning method.
