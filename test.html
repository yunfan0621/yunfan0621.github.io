About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan. I am advised by Professor Jia Deng.

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering: Systems from the University of Michigan in 2013, and my B.S. degree in Electronics Engineering from National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2009. During the summer/fall of 2013, I was a visiting student researcher at Stanford University, under the supervision of Professor Silvio Savarese. From 2010 to 2011, I worked as a research assistant at the Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan, where I was advised by Dr. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.
